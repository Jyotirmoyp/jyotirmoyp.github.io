# Seismic wave propagation

## Surface to Surface travel time 

$$
T(p) = 2 \int_{0}^{z_p} \frac{u^2(z)}{\sqrt{u^2(z) - p^2}} \, dz
= 2 \int_{0}^{z_p} \frac{u^2(z)}{\eta} \, dz
$$

## Rang 

$$
X(p) = 2 \int_{0}^{z_p} \frac{1}{\sqrt{u^2(z) - p^2}} \, dz
$$
